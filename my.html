Syllabus of Summer School

Introduction to the summer school

The summer school covers several recent advances in the topic of distributed control, optimization and learning. The main object of the course is to present in an accessible way (lectures, tutorial/seminars) to graduate students some advanced control and
optimization methods for large-scale systems that arise in modern control engineering and data science. The content of the course covers basics for convex optimization, first-order optimization methods (e.g., gradient method, stochastic method, accelerated gradient
methods and primal-dual methods), decomposition and splitting methods (e.g., dual decomposition, monotone operators and operator splitting, ADMM), as well as recent developed parallel and distributed algorithms. Besides, several tutorial/seminars from leading
research scholars will be provided to introduce some frontier advanced topics in distributed control, optimization and learning. To enhance the learning outcome of students, new online learning models, such as SPOC, will be likely employed to promote self-learning
and diversity of learning processes, along with a bunch of concrete examples including smart grid, sensor networks and machine learning for the sake of enriching the content of the course. After completing this course, the students are expected to be able to apply
the control and optimization techniques learned from this course to large-scale cyber-physical systems as well as other related research areas.

Course Content

Lectures

  • Day 1. Introduction to the course

    • Background • Graph Basics and Weight Matrix • (Dynamic) Average Consensus

    • Convex Set • (Non)-Convex Function • Smoothness • Strong Convexity • Strong Duality • Slater Condition • KKT

  • Day 2. Convex Optimization

    • (Unconstrained) Convex Optimization Problem • Gradient Method • Convergence Property and Analysis

  • Day 3. Stochastic Optimization

    • Stochastic Gradient Methods • Convergence Analysis • Variance Reduced Methods: SAG, SAGA, SVRG…

  • Day 4. Distributed Convex Optimization

    • Finite-Sum Problem • Distributed Optimization • Distributed Algorithms: DGD, Gradient Tracking, Push-Pull/SONATA, Primal-Dual Methods • Connection to Variance Reduction

  • Day 5. Advanced Topics in (Distributed) Optimization

    • Composite Optimization • Proximal/Projected Gradient Methods • Accelerated (Distributed) Methods • Proximal Point Method • Monotone Operators • ADMM

Way of Teaching

  • Lectures (focused on (distributed) optimization) + Tutorials/Seminars (covering frontier advanced topics on distributed control, optimization and learning)

  • Online (Key concepts; sketch of proofs) + offline (mathematical derivation)
